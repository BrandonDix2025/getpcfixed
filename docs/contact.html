Common questions
Is GetPCFixed really free? ▼
Yes — the core app is completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no tricks. We may offer optional premium features in the future but the core will always be free.
Will it touch my PC without asking? ▼
Never. GetPCFixed only makes changes when you explicitly approve them. Every single time, without exception. That's our core promise.
Can I undo a fix? ▼
Yes. Every fix is logged and reversible with one click. If something doesn't feel right after a change, you can undo it instantly from your fix history.
What Windows versions are supported? ▼
GetPCFixed supports Windows 10 and Windows 11. We recommend keeping Windows updated for the best experience.
